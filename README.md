## 🌟 About ARC-SJEC

The **Automation & Robotics Club (ARC-SJEC)** is a multidisciplinary, student-driven technical organization at **St. Joseph Engineering College, Mangaluru**.

Established in **2016**, ARC-SJEC serves as a platform for students to transform theoretical knowledge into **real-world engineering solutions** through hands-on development, collaborative innovation, and structured project workflows.

We focus on building a strong foundation in modern engineering domains aligned with **Industry 4.0 technologies**.

---

## ⚙️ Core Domains

- 🤖 Robotics  
- ⚡ Automation Systems  
- 📡 Internet of Things (IoT)  
- 🧠 Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning  
- 🔌 Embedded Systems
